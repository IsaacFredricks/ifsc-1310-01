  1. How do I use the Segway Centaur?

    It's rather quite simple really. Take a seat, power on the Centaur, and use the throttle handle and start driving away!

  2. What is the minimum age to use the Centaur?

    Minumum age to set up is 18. Minumum age to drive is 12.

  3. My Centaur was broken during shipping. Can I get a refund?

    No, you cannot.

  4. Can I take my Centaur on the highway?

    Not if you don't want to get seriously injured.

  5. Where can I buy one?

    At a Kmart near you!

  6. What is the refund policy?

    You cannot refund 24 hours after purchase.

  7. What batteries does the Segway Centaur use?

    The Centaur uses standard car batteries.

  8. Where do I go to get replacement batteries?

    Go to a local car dealership or go to our official store to purchase one.
